Facilities & Amenities

Yashoda Hospitals in-patient accommodation is hygienic and spacious with super specialty standards. We offer premier facilities for in-patients ranging from tastefully decorated A/C Deluxe rooms to single private rooms, shared rooms, cubicles, general wards and VIP Suites. Our private rooms provide en-suite bathroom, telephone, and television. All rooms are well ventilated with all modern amenities like telephone, closet for personal belongings, cable TV, hot & cold water, etc.

Hospital rooms are generally assigned based upon the patients request, insurance limitations and type of care the physician recommends.
